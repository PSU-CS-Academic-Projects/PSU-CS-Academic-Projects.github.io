About Us

Building Tomorrow's
Software Today

PSU-CS-Academic-Projects is a GitHub organization dedicated to housing and showcasing the computer science coursework and personal projects of Palawan State University students.

We believe in open-source learning — sharing code, collaborating across courses, and building a portfolio that speaks louder than a résumé.
